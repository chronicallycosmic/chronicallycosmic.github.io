Black Phantasm

Fall Look One

Growing accustomed to the monotonous events of everyday life is the kind of lost I never want to be. I want to get lost in the unknown, the spontaneous, and the strange. After the mundane events of an average school day, I ventured out to a myriad of lands and experienced a thousand different versions of myself. That is what a photoshoot can feel like, when done inexorably and wholeheartedly. The venue I explored, along with my brilliant photographer, was Downtown Blacksburg. Although underrated and often underappreciated, there are some gemlike locations yet to be uncovered in its forgotten alleys and corners. Art is everywhere. I find it in the spray-painted graffiti on the brick walls, the wispy clouds in the clear cerulean sky, and in the expressions of the passerby’s walking along the sunlit streets. Every moment in time and every snapshot in reality is a painting in the gallery of this universe. Photography is an attempt to capture these metaphysical frames in a tangible form. Dressing in all black makes me feel absolutely unstoppable. Black is my favorite color because it is the color of the universe, and when I wear this majestic color, I feel as if I have blended into the cosmos and become one with that profound vastness. For this day, I chose a casual streetwear outfit, comprised of a thin sweater with the lettering “deadly” painted across both sleeves and a spiked leather vest. The centerpiece of this outfit is the black beanie embellished with an alien symbol, further representing my fascination with outer space. I would characterize the aura of this outfit as harboring a relaxed futuristic vibe, like an alien trying to adjust to earthly fashion.
